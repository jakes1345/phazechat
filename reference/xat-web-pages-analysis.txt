8. KEY BUSINESS RULES
========================================================================

POWERS:
  - Require active subscription (days) to function
  - Prices fluctuate based on demand
  - Tradable between users
  - Can be turned on/off individually
  - Each has an associated smiley
  - Non-refundable
  - Latest power: ID 741 (LIMITED)

ACES (subscription-based):
  - Require days to function
  - Time-limited subscriptions
  - Auto-renew option
  - Level-up discount system
  - Cannot be traded
  - Not included in Everypower
  - 5 ace types: yellow(700), iconcolor(702), pawnhue(728), xaf(736), vaf(738)

SHORT NAMES:
  - 4-9 characters
  - Only one per person
  - Unused 90 days may be resold
  - Costs vary
  - Replaces current name

AUCTIONS:
  - Must claim within 12 hours of winning
  - Bids are binding
  - Mistake: lose 20% (must notify immediately)
  - Moving xats away after winning: lose 100%
  - Name auctions: old name is lost

GROUPS:
  - Must be ownerless 3+ months to transfer
  - Email set to buyer's
  - Inappropriate names may be deleted without refund

PROMOTIONS:
  - Age 14+ content only
  - Must be moderated
  - No rank sales
  - No religious/political content
  - Minimum 0.5 hours
  - Can pay with xats or days
  - Banner ads: 728x90 pixels, image authorization costs 50 xats
