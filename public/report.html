Emergency Safety

If someone is in immediate danger or you are experiencing a genuine emergency, please contact your local emergency services directly. xat cannot respond to emergencies in real time.

What Happens Next?
  1. Your report is sent to the xat moderation team.
  2. A moderator reviews the report within 48 hours.
  3. If a violation is found, appropriate action is taken (warn, ban, or escalate).
  4. Repeated or severe violations may result in a permanent ban.
