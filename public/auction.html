How Auctions Work

  • Short name auctions run for a set period (typically 3–7 days).
  • The highest bidder at the end of the auction wins the short name.
  • Xats are held in escrow when you bid. If outbid, your xats are immediately returned.
  • The winning bidder receives the short name assigned to a group of their choice.
  • Short names won at auction cannot be transferred or re-auctioned for 30 days.
  • xat reserves the right to cancel any auction if the name is flagged as inappropriate.
