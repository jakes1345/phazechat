Short Names

Get a Short Name for Your Group

Short names are easy-to-remember URLs for your xat chat group.

What is a Short Name?

A short name gives your group a custom, easy-to-remember URL. Instead of sharing a long URL with a group ID, you can share a clean link like:

xat.com/YourGroupName

How Short Names Work

On this server, short names are automatically assigned when you create a group. Simply navigate to any valid group name URL and the group will be created automatically.

Short Name Rules

  • 1-20 characters long
  • Letters, numbers, and underscores only
  • Not case-sensitive (Chat = chat = CHAT)
  • Cannot be a reserved word (login, store, powers, etc.)
