Safety Center

Staying Safe on xat

xat is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. Here are some important safety tips:

IMPORTANT: We will NEVER ask for your email password. Only use your email password to log in to your email provider.

Protect Your Account

  • Never share your password with anyone, including people claiming to be xat staff.
  • Use a strong password that is unique to xat.
  • Be careful of phishing — always verify you are on the correct xat server before entering credentials.
  • Log out when using shared computers.

Protect Your Personal Information

  • Never share your real name, address, phone number, or school with strangers.
  • Be cautious about sharing photos or personal details in chat.
  • Don't click suspicious links sent by other users.

Trading Safety

  • Only trade through the official xat trade system.
  • Never give items first in a "trust trade."
  • Be wary of deals that seem too good to be true.
  • Report scammers to a moderator immediately.

Reporting

If you encounter any of the following, please report it immediately:

  • Harassment or bullying
  • Inappropriate content
  • Scams or fraud
  • Users under 13 years old
  • Any illegal activity

To report, contact a moderator in the Help chat or use the report function in any chat group.

For Parents

xat is designed for users 13 and older. We recommend that parents:

  • Talk to their children about online safety
  • Monitor their children's online activity
  • Report any concerns to xat staff
