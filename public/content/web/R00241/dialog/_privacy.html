privacy policy
1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your username, email address, and any messages or content you post. We also collect technical data such as IP addresses, browser type, and device information to operate and improve the service.

2. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your session, store your preferences, and improve service performance. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookie settings through your browser or through the privacy preferences panel.

  • Necessary cookies — required for the service to function. These cannot be disabled.
  • Performance cookies — used to measure and improve service performance.
  • Third-party content cookies — set when you open group tabs that load external content.
3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Provide and operate the chat service
  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your session
  • Prevent abuse and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Improve service performance and reliability
  • Communicate service updates or important notices
4. Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data with service providers who assist us in operating the service, subject to confidentiality agreements. When you open group tabs or embedded content, those third-party sites may set their own cookies and collect data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active, or as required by law. Chat logs may be retained for a limited period for moderation purposes.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please use the contact form on the site.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of data transmitted to or from the service.

8. Children's Privacy

The service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us for removal.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please use the contact form on the site.
