Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When you use xat, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Username, password (hashed), registration date, and user ID.
  • Chat Data: Messages sent in chat rooms are transmitted in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about which chat rooms you visit and your online status.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to maintain your session and preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the xat chat service
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and community guidelines
  • Improve and optimize our service

3. Cookies

xat uses the following types of cookies:

  • Necessary Cookies: Required for the service to function (session management, authentication).
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the service.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Used by group tabs and embedded content.

You can manage your cookie preferences using the cookie settings available at the bottom of any page.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Service providers who help us operate xat

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. Chat messages are transmitted in real-time and are not permanently stored. You may request deletion of your account by contacting an administrator.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Object to processing of your data

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, visit the Help chat group or contact an administrator.
