Become an Ace!

Aces are premium xat members who get exclusive benefits and recognition.

Ace Badge

Display a golden star badge next to your name in chat, showing your Ace status to everyone.

Priority Support

Get faster support and assistance from the xat team when you need help.

Exclusive Features

Access special features and content only available to Ace members.

How to Become an Ace

To become an Ace, you need:

  • A registered xat account with days (subscription)
  • A minimum balance of xats and days
  • Good standing in the xat community

Ace status is automatically applied when your account meets the requirements. Keep your days active to maintain your Ace status.
